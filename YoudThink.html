I take my digital camera everywhere, everywhere.  But I made the conscious decision to leave it at home this afternoon when I went for a haircut.

So please close your eyes and imagine six window cleaners free-rappeling down a 30-story building.

And see me, the crazy baby-talking American, accosting a young Chinese man wearing a Green Bay Packers jacket and see his baffled look as I try to explain that Green Bay is where I grew up in America.

**************************

You know, I'd read that the Chinese refer to us as "big noses".  Well, I've been looking into mostly nothing but Chinese faces for so long now that, when I saw a Westerner on the way back from the barbershop, the thought popped into my mind: "He sure has a big nose".  Guess I need to check the mirror a little more closely when I shave tomorrow.
