Visit Number One was back during Spring Festival.  You might remember the endless stairs on the way up to the highest temple at LaoShan.

Visit Number Two was when Karen and I went to Taiqing Temple, which is the lowest temple at LaoShan.  It's almost on the beach, so, in that instance, we had to walk down a ways from the bus stop.

Visit Number Three was this last Sunday.  Amanda, my former Chinese tutor, and Da Peng, her husband, arranged for me to join their trekking group for an outing.
Here are our leaders.  They both were very friendly and spoke excellent English.
We started out on the same road that I had taken during Spring Festival.
