Feng Shui And The Pixiu
(conclusion)		 BACK TO DATE PAGE
The Pixiu generates good Feng Shui and can guard your house and family members from evil spirits or catastrophe and also ensures good health to everyone in the house.

And the Pixiu will also ward off evil and wicked people who intend to harm you or your family.  It also helps you through obstacles and hard times in life.

But, most interestingly, the Pixiu does not have an anus, so whatever good fortune it absorbs will be trapped in its stomach for your use.  None of the good fortune will be lost once it is in your Pixiu's stomach.
