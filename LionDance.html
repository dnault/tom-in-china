We, like much of China, work Saturday mornings. On the way to the bus stop one Saturday noon, my co-workers and I ran into these two fellows warming up to perform at a wedding.
That's when I learned that, what I had always thought were dragons, are actually lions.  It turns out that the Chinese Lion Dance goes back some one thousand years.  Since the lions express joy and happiness, they are popular at weddings.
