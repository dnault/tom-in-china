Soft Sleeper / Hard Sleeper
(continuation)
So what's the difference between a 'hard' sleeper and a 'soft' sleeper besides the obvious fact that the hard sleeper mattress is thinner?  It's six people in a hard sleeper in the same cubic space as four people in a soft sleeper--three bunks up each side spaced closer together rather than two bunks farther apart--and there is no door for compartmental privacy.

In other words, the hard sleeper has the potential to be a lot more interesting, especially since the bottom bunk is considered communal seating unless the person assigned the bunk is actually sleeping in it.
The man in the middle.
