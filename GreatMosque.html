One of the largest mosques in China, the Great Mosque in Xi'an is a blend of Chinese and Islamic architecture.
Facing west (towards Mecca) instead of the usual south for a temple in China, the mosque begins with a typical Chinese temple feature--the spirit wall--designed to keep demons at bay.
