A final shot as we worked our way back to the entrance of this enchanted temple.
And, to top it off, this kindly lady sat down by us as we rested in the shade and told our guide to take good care of us as guests of China.

It turned out that she was even a tourist from Qingdao, so we had something in common to talk about.  I have my reservations about governments--U.S. as well as others--but I have no reservations about common people--we're all in this together.
