Bricks And Blocks
This broken bit of masonry caught my eye the other day as I was looking out the bus window stopped in traffic on the way home during rush hour.  It got me to thinking.

Qingdao is a city of blocks, brick, and stone.  Sidewalks are not poured concrete, for example.  Instead, they're made of tiles placed tightly together on a layer of sand or cemented into place.

So, when the time comes to rip up a sidewalk and bury new pipes, they stack up all the tiles to the side and start digging.
