Chicken Eggs
(conclusion)
This is a neighborhood stand that I pass whenever I take the alley short-cut between the bus stop and the Office.

There were bags of bulk rice, beans, and seeds on the ledge the day I took this picture, but no eggs.
This morning, though, right on cue, there they were.  The stand offers a surprising range of items, and you'll notice that it also serves as the local public telephone booth.
