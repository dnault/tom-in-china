Chinese God of Prosperity BACK TO DATE PAGE
If I'm not mistaken, this is a representation of Lu, the Chinese god of prosperity.  He is the god of high-rank affluence, and he symbolizes the opportunity to better oneself and receive high rewards.

I have seen this deity represented in several company owners' offices, but this is the first time that I have actually  photographed it.  The factory tour guide, on second thought, wasn't very happy that I had taken this picture, but I had asked permission beforehand, and I wasn't told to erase it, which I would have done upon request.

The traditional gods are alive and well in China.
