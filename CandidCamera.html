Candid Camera BACK TO DATE PAGE
This picture was taken today, May 26th in Beijing.  I know I owe you Taishan, the Forbidden City, the Great Wall, and the Summer Palace, but today was the Temple of Heaven, Tiananmen Square, and the Snack Street--our second truly physical day in a row in 85-degree-plus heat.

We were trying to sit down on a narrow ledge along the sidewalk after a long day, when Karen toppled backward onto the grass and just lay there and my unforgiving camera snapped this picture.  I succeeded in not laughing, but she knows me and accused me of having taken a picture.  I confessed.

So now join me in laughing.
