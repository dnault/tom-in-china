I took this picture soon after the start of the Lunar New Year celebration.  This scene originally caught my attention because of the broom, but I remember thinking to myself that the discarded fireworks box sure looked like it would make a great rocket launcher.

I didn't think anything more of it at the time and continued my walk that afternoon, which resulted in the pictures of kite-flying down by the waterfront that I posted the next day.

Well, I didn't know it then, but I was on to something.
