If I'd stepped into this manhole just to the left of the sidewalk, it would have swallowed my whole leg and I still wouldn't have hit bottom.

My Chinese friends have the refreshing attitude that anyone who steps into a hole like this gets what he deserves and must either be blind, or stupid, or both.
I've been warned not to step on top of any manhole cover because they've been known to collapse underfoot.
