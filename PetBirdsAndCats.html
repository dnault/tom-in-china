Pet Birds And Cats
A pet bird is a pet bird everywhere and so is a pet cat, but I've been collecting pictures of them anyway and would like to share a few of them with you today. 
I've occasionally mentioned the alley short-cut that I take between the bus stop and the Office.  These two fellows are often just singing away in the morning.
Pet birds is kind of an old-man thing around here.  They take the birds along to the park where the old men sit and chat with friends while the birds sing.
This lady is selling birds.
