Most of the vehicles on today's posting are people-powered or just seemed weird to me.  I'll introduce you to hand vehicles, mopeds, scooters, and maybe even to trucks and semi-trailers another time.

I've whittled this down to 96 pictures, so please bear with me--it could've been a lot longer.  There are redundancies, but I have my favorites, and I hope that you might also find the various backgrounds to be of interest.

In any event, if you want a sobering moment, stop and think about the majority of your fellow human beings on this planet who do not own a car like you do, much less two cars, and certainly not a speedboat or purely recreational four-wheeler.
