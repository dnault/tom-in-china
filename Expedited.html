When I saw UPS, DHL, and FedEx slugging it out over here for the expedited delivery market, I read it as a vestige of U.S. international economic power and creativity .  I'm not well-informed, but Sino-Trans is the only Chinese company of significance that I'm aware of that has a relationship with one of our Big Three, in their case DHL.

UPS is currently winning the eyeball war in Qingdao--they're the only one that I've noticed with billboard advertising--but DHL is winning as the carrier of choice in our Office.
This billboard shows UPS delivering the Olympics, which is a real hot button around here
