Bare Baby Butts
There's a slit on some toddlers' outer clothes that I'm sure I'd never've noticed if a fellow from Singapore I ran into on a factory visit south of Shanghai hadn't asked me "What do you think of those bare baby butts?  They swear babies' butts don't freeze."  Well, I must admit, I'd never thought about bare baby butts before, frozen or otherwise.

But that slit in the outerwear made a lot of sense when I started to pay attention to parents changing their children's disposable diapers in public without undressing them.

Think about it.
