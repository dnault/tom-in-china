Not all entranceways are guarded, but those that are still catch my eye.  We'll start with the mythical and traditional and end with the whimsical.
The Ki'lin is a mythical being of Chinese mythology comparable to the western unicorn.  They personify all that is good, pure, and peaceful.  Ki'lin live in paradise and only visit the world at the birth of a wise philosopher.  The male holds a ball under his paw and the female holds a playful cub.
I walk past this handsome couple every day.  That's my apartment building in the background on the right.
