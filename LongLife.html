I spotted these cigarettes in a Duty Free shop at the airport as I left Taiwan.
I'm not sure whether this is the size carton we're used to in the States, but this carton costs $11.50, which has to impress those of you who smoke with how much "sin tax" you're paying.

I'd like to join the Marketing Department of the manufacturer of these cigarettes in wishing a Long Life to those of you who smoke.

But my wish comes from the heart.  And I think you'll agree that theirs comes from the wallet.

Unfortunately, China still smokes way too much.
