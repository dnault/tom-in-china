Kunming Lake occupies three-quarters of the park and you can see only half of its expanse on this map.  Using the map for reference, we entered the park from the far right center and walked from right to left along the shore.  And then we took a boat ride.
We weren't the only lao ren enjoying the Summer Palace's respite from the 90-degree-plus temperatures in Beijing.
