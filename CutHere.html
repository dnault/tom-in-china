My Chinese co-worker Fish and I took this plane from Qingdao to Shanghai on December 5th to start a nine-day trip visiting existing and potential suppliers.




This is a close-up of the window under the letter A in AIRLINES.



We both had never seen this before and had a good chuckle.
I've only been on two domestic flights, but both planes seemed quite new.

Airport security is the same as we have in the States, including what is allowed and not allowed in carry-on luggage.
In the course of this nine-day trip, I took 2,964 pictures.  I'll trash at least half of them because they'll either be out of focus, or the bus window will've been too dirty, or I'll have completely missed whatever it was I was trying to capture.  But, as always, my biggest challenge will be to decide what to share with you.  I simply cannot share it all.
