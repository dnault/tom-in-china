My life's path led this American to China on October 17th, 2007, when I arrived in Qingdao (cheeng-dao) on a long-term business assignment.

This website has allowed me to share the adventure with family and friends through daily postings in pictures and words. 

I left China on July 16th, 2008, with mixed emotions.  I left behind a part of myself, but I've replaced it with a part of China that makes me excited about the future.
