Children
The following pictures were taken between November 20th and March 16th, so you will see a variety of outerwear.  They are in chronological order with the exception of the picture below.

Most of the pictures were taken on the sly, so there are a lot of side and back views as well as shots from moving buses.  As for the pictures when the Mom or Dad knew I was taking them, you will rarely see the children smiling.  I asked my Chinese friends and they explained the obvious--my round-eyed, big-nosed face was scary.
