Karen and I've taken you to the jade store, the cloisonne store, and the fresh-water pearl store, all in Beijing, where we said "No, no, no!"  We finally broke down in Xi'an, the last stop of our trip, as you've already seen when we searched for the Great Mosque through that warren of incredible shops.
This is where that break-down began.  It was our first morning in Xi'an, and we were on the way to see the Terracotta Warriors.  The folks at this store are actually manufacturers, and they would have you believe that they are strictly following the archaeologically-discovered processes to create modern replicas of the Warriors.
