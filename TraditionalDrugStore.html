David was looking for a specific over-the-counter cold remedy to take back to Singapore.  We'd been unsuccessful in four drug stores before coming here.  It was Sunday evening.  We had gone to the health club for the Spinning (bicycling) class, and I'd taken my first Taiji class.  Held my own in Taiji, but also enjoyed an introduction to Taiji sword.  Then I'd hosted David at Qingdao's authentic Russian restaurant--something we'd been meaning to do for almost two weeks since he'd returned from Singapore.  We were out walking off our dinner after dark.
These traditional drug stores had been pointed out to me before, but this was my first time inside.  We entered under these beautiful wooden and painted glass lanterns.
